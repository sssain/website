About Me

I'm Naufal Zaqie — 17 years old, a front-end web developer with a deep love for turning ideas into beautiful, interactive experiences.
My camera is my second set of eyes. Whether I’m framing a shot or editing a video, I’m always searching for rhythm — in the way light
cuts through a window, in how movement slows or speeds across a frame. All my passions — from sketching pixels in Figma to capturing sunlit
streets through a lens — are just different expressions of the same thing: storytelling through rhythm, emotion, and art.
